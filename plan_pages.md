# Bewild Website Page Plan

## Page Structure (from https://bewild.life/)

1. **Home Page**
   - Hero Banner
   - Shop Categories
   - Sustainability & Farming Practices
   - Why Choose BeWild
   - Meet Our Experts
   - Footer

2. **Shop Page**
   - All Products (Coffee, Grains, Spices, Condiments)
   - Filters by category
   - Product cards

3. **Product Details Page**
   - Product description
   - Benefits
   - Ingredients (if any)
   - Add to cart

4. **About Us**
   - Brand story
   - Our vision
   - Our farming principles

5. **Sustainability Page**
   - Organic practices
   - Eco initiatives
   - Farmer stories

6. **Contact Page**
   - Form
   - Contact Info
   - FAQ (Optional)

7. **Blog Page** (Optional/Coming Soon)
   - Articles on farming, recipes, sustainability

8. **Cart / Checkout Pages**
   - User-friendly layout
   - Minimal distractions
